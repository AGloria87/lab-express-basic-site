About

John Dyer Baizley was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, on October 10, 1977. He sings and plays guitar for the band metal band Baroness. Not only is he a musician, but he also creates the album artwork for Baroness, and has also done album art for other bands. Baroness started in 2003 in Savanah, Georgia, and has released five studio albums, all of which use colors as their titles and themes.

On August 15, 2012, while the band was on tour, they were involved in a road accident when the bus in which the band were traveling fell from a viaduct in England. Most of the band members suffered injuries from the accident, with John Baizley having a broken arm and a broken leg. Despite the accident, they continued planning tours and were performing again soon after their recovery.
